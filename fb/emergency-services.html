Shocking Discovery

The Missing Consultation

While Swanage Town Council pushes through their controversial Shore Road changes, we've uncovered a dangerous oversight: they NEVER consulted the South West Ambulance Trust about how emergency vehicles would access our community.

That's right - NO ONE asked the ambulance service how their multi-million pound infrastructure plan would affect emergency response times to YOUR home, YOUR family, YOUR neighbors.

But they did remember to design improvements that boost revenue to Council-owned assets. Priorities much?

The Consequences

Lives at Risk

Emergency response isn't a luxury - it's a lifeline. When Shore Road closes, ambulances will face the same traffic chaos as everyone else, navigating through congested Victorian streets never designed for emergency vehicles.

The summer tourist season makes everything worse. When 180,000 visitors flood Swanage, every minute counts. Blocked roads don't just cause inconvenience - they can be the difference between life and death.
